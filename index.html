The Computer Society of Anna University is the oldest and largest technical society of College of Engineering, Guindy. It was originally started to foster innovation and creativity among the students in the field of Computer Science and Information Technology. Over the years, it has seen a multitude of constructive changes and has now attained the status of being the most accepted society among the student's circle in terms of exhibiting and sharing the knowledge of innovative cutting-edge technology. The most celebrated organization of the campus today, the CSAU aims high in terms of quality and success.

The CSAU provides activities galore to the student community testing their skills in various arenas and a treasure trove of opportunities in the most exciting ways possible. To list a few, EUREKA - the very first traditional event of the CSAU every academic year conducted by the second years for the first years fresh from school, a real ice-breaker; BRAIN SCAN - an extremely exciting event with puzzles, math and logic enough to get any student’s brain whizzing into action.

About Sparks

SPARKS, the fairy of festivities is here, weaving her magic all around!! In order to bridge the gap between high school students and engineering students, we have been conducting an event called SPARKS from the year 1991, exclusively for school students.Since its inception in 1991, SPARKS has seen a huge participation from school students from top-notch schools. With exciting events like Quiz, Impromptu, Innovate, Web Weave, Dumb C, Speakathon and Mr. and Miss. SPARKS on the list, SPARKS aims at providing extreme fun to school students casting an almost feverish aura for a full day in the campus grounds!
